# Knowledge Notes

## Core Heuristics

1. Start with direction before explanation.
2. Separate price action from narrative drivers.
3. Treat volume spikes as context, not proof.
4. Compare each stock to a small sector peer group.
5. Call out uncertainty when the move depends on one short-lived event.

## Reading Guide

- Uptrend: higher highs, higher lows, and support above a recent average.
- Sideways: mixed direction with weak follow-through.
- Weakening trend: failed breakout or repeated rejection near the same level.

## Risk Framing

- Macro headlines can overwhelm company-specific signals.
- Currency moves can change export sentiment quickly.
- A strong recent rally can still produce a weak near-term entry.

## Output Style

- Lead with one sentence on the current trend.
- Follow with 2 to 4 bullets on evidence.
- End with a short watch item for the next update.
